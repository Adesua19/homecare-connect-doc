# HomeCare Connect

**Connecting Busy Families with Trusted Household Help**

---

## What Is This Project?

HomeCare Connect is a mobile app that helps busy people find reliable household workers for cleaning, cooking, laundry, and errands. Think of it like Uber, but for household chores.

### The Problem

A pregnant mother told me: *"I haven't been able to get anyone to help me do these chores."*

Many busy people face this problem:
- Parents juggling work and childcare
- Professionals with no time for housework
- Students focusing on their studies
- Elderly people who need help at home

At the same time, many domestic workers struggle to find steady jobs and build their reputation.

### The Solution

HomeCare Connect brings these two groups together safely and easily through a mobile app.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Verified Workers
Every service provider goes through:
- ID verification
- Background checks
- Reference checks (for experienced workers)
- Skills tests (for newcomers)

### 2. Easy Booking
- Choose your service (cleaning, cooking, laundry, errands)
- Pick your date and time
- See available workers near you
- Book with one tap

### 3. Safe Payments
- Pay through the app (no cash needed)
- Money held safely until job is done
- Automatic payment to workers after completion

### 4. Trust & Safety
- GPS tracking when worker is on the job
- In-app messaging
- 24/7 emergency hotline
- Two-way ratings and reviews

---

## How It Works

### For Families:
1. Create an account
2. Choose the service you need
3. Pick a date and time
4. See available workers and their ratings
5. Book your choice
6. Pay through the app
7. Worker shows up and does the job
8. Rate your experience

### For Service Providers:
1. Apply to join
2. Complete verification process
3. Set your availability and rates
4. Receive booking requests
5. Accept jobs that fit your schedule
6. Complete the work
7. Get paid to your wallet
8. Build your reputation with good ratings

## Technology Used

### Mobile App
- **React Native** - Build one app that works on both iPhone and Android
- **Expo** - Makes app development faster and easier

### Backend (Server)
- **Node.js** - Runs the server
- **Express** - Handles web requests
- **MongoDB** - Stores all our data
- **Redis** - Makes the app faster with caching

### Payment & Services
- **Paystack** - Processes payments safely
- **Google Maps** - Shows locations and calculates distances
- **Twilio** - Sends SMS notifications
- **Firebase** - Handles user login and push notifications

## Revenue Model

How the platform makes money:

1. **Service Fee**: 15-20% commission on each booking
2. **Subscriptions**: Premium plans for frequent users
3. **Verification Fees**: One-time fee for worker background checks
4. **Premium Listings**: Featured placement for top-rated workers
